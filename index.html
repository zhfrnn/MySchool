Muhammadiyah 3 Junior Highschool of Yogyakarta

Muhammadiyah 3 Junior Highschool of Yogyakarta, this school is one of the best Muhammadiyah schools in Yogyakarta. located on Captain Piere Tendean street, Wirobrajan, Yogyakarta City, Special Region of Yogyakarta. This school was built on 14 July 1951, this year's principal is Mr. Susamta S.Pd, M.Pd. A few facts about him, he used to be a subject teacher at this school, then he moved to another school and then came back to this school as a headmaster.

The area of ​​this school used to be 1183m², but now the area has increased to 4123m². This school has three opposite buildings, the main building is used for grades 7th and 8th, the second building is used for grade 9th, while the third building is for sports fields. There are 27 classrooms, 7th grade is from class A–G, 8th grade is from class A–G as well, and 9th grade is from class A–I. Has 1 science laboratory, 1 art room, 1 music studio, 1 hall, 1 minimarket, 2 canteens, and 2 libraries. Class outings, study tours, competitions, discussion activities, socialization, and many more. The agenda currently being carried out is sharing takjil, and distributing zakat to people, these activities are carried out in the context of Ramadan this year. This school has achieved many achievements, both from the students and the school itself. The achievement that this school just achieved was the first place in the green school competition on 2022.
