Vision SMP Muhammadiyah 3 Yogyakarta :

"FAITHFUL, EXCELLENT IN ACHIEVEMENT, CULTURED, AND ENVIRONMENTALLY INSIGHTFUL‚Äù

The mission of SMP Muhammadiyah 3 Yogyakarta :

1. Carry out training to increase faith and piety through habituation,
includes:
a) Tadarus,
b) Dhuha prayer, midday prayer, Asr prayer in congregation schools,
c) Learning Iqra and Tahfidz, d)
Implementation of PHBI.

2. Carry out effective, creative and innovative learning and guidance refers to the Education Unit Level Curriculum (KTSP) and Curriculum Operational Education Unit (KOSP).

3. Improving the quality of educators and education staff in the framework of service Education at school.

4. Increase the competence of students in the academic field. 5. Maximizing extracurricular activities in the field of religion, scientific work, arts, and sports according to the potential, talents, and interests of students.

6. Carry out specialization programs for IT and Bilingual (English) classes as school flagship program.

7. Applying local cultural wisdom values.

8. Carry out the use of Javanese gagrak clothing and Javanese language every Thursday Pahing.

9. Implementing a population alert school.

10. Develop an environmentally sound school curriculum.

11. Carry out environmental awareness awareness activities through activities 9K (Security, cleanliness, order, beauty, kinship, shade, Health, Openness, exemplary) so that schools with insight are realized environment.
